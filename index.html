Pikachu

Otherwise known as Ri's "Little Buddy" Pikachu has been with Ri since the age of 6 and the two have been inseperable ever since.

Oshawott

The second(technically first) to join Ri in his adventure as a Pokemon trainer, Oshawott was very against being with humans at first due to how he ended up in his postion, but soon grew too like Ri.

Dewott

After enough training Oshawott has now become a Dewott. Now with two shells insted of one he has more options than he did in the past. But this landmark won't stop him from growing even stronger.

Samurott

The final Evolution of Oshawott, Samurott is a stronger and more serious version of himself. The two swords that come from his arms are his greatest weapons. one really good hit and you're out for a while.

Riolu and Zorua

These two are inseparable. Riolu refuses to let Zorua out of his site and will protect him at all cost. After Ri finds them badly hurt and gives them some items to heal with, the two secretly follow him and eventually join him in hopes of becoming stronger.

Lucario and Zoroark

These Two have finally achived their goal to become stronger. Lucario and Zoroark now both evolved can take care of themselfs and destroy their enemies in the process.

Cimpchar

The energetic chimp, Chimpchar. When Chimpchar and Ri first met, Chimpchar just sort of liked Ri the moment he saw him and soon became the 5th member of the team.

Monferno

After enough training Chimpchar became strong enough to really pack a punch. With his new fighting ability Monferno isn't a mon you'd wanna mess with.

Infernape

This chimp has reach his true potential! Infernape has all the strength needed to win any challange thrown at him. All because of his will to never give up and the STRENGTH OF HIS INNER FLAME!!!
